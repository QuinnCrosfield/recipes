Ingredients:
 - 1/2 cup peanut butter
 - 2 slices of bread
 - 1/2 cup jelly

Directions:
  1. Spread peanut butter evenly on one side of one of the slices of bread.
  2. Spread jelly on one side of the other slice of bread.
  3. Finally put the sandwich together by placing the jelly side onto the peanut butter side.
